BANAUE

Banaue rice terraces

Ancient, UNESCO-listed terraces carved into the mountains by the Ifugao people, often referred to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World."

FALLS

Tappiya falls

A majestic waterfall near the Banaue Rice Terraces, offering a refreshing swim and picturesque views.

MOUNT

Mt. Amuyao

One of the highest peaks in Luzon, popular for its challenging hikes and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

TAMAN

Tam-an village

A traditional Ifugao village where visitors can experience the local culture, crafts, and way of life.

MUSEUM

Banaue museum

A repository of Ifugao history, artifacts, and cultural exhibits, providing insight into the region's rich heritage.

GUIHOB

Guihob natural pool

A serene natural pool fed by cool mountain springs, perfect for relaxation and a refreshing dip.

BOGYAH

Bogyah hot spring

A natural hot spring ideal for soaking and unwinding, surrounded by lush greenery.

CAVE

Balangagan cave

An intriguing cave system with impressive rock formations, stalactites, and stalagmites, offering adventurous spelunking experiences.

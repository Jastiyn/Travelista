FORT

Fort santiago

A citadel built by Spanish conquistador Miguel López de Legazpi; it served as a defense fortress and now houses the Rizal Shrine Museum.

CHURCH

San agustin church and museum

The oldest stone church in the Philippines, a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring a museum with religious artifacts and colonial-era treasures.

Plaza

Plaza de roma

A central public square in Intramuros, historically used for civic events and surrounded by significant landmarks like Manila Cathedral.

Casa

Casa manila museum

A reconstructed 19th-century Spanish colonial house showcasing period furniture, decor, and lifestyle of the colonial elite.

garden

Puerta real gardens

A beautiful garden area used for events and recreation, offering a serene escape within the historic walls of Intramuros.

Bahay

Bahay tsinoy

A museum dedicated to the history and contributions of the Chinese community in the Philippines, featuring exhibits on Chinese-Filipino heritage.

Baluartillo

Baluartillo de san francisco javier

A fortified structure that was part of the defense system of Intramuros, now an interesting historical site for visitors.

museum

Rizal shrine museum

Located within Fort Santiago, this museum is dedicated to the life and works of the Philippine national hero, Dr. José Rizal.
